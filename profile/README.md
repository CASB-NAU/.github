## We make living cells computable.

<table>
<tr>
<td width="64%" valign="top">

<h3>农业生命系统的建模、预测与理性设计</h3>

<p>我们在<strong>合成生物学、系统生物学与机器学习</strong>的交汇处开展研究：在计算机中重建作物相关微生物与植物细胞，模拟它们在遗传扰动与环境胁迫下的行为，并让预测结果反过来指导湿实验。</p>

<p>We reconstruct crop-associated microbes and plant cells <em>in silico</em>, predict their responses to genetic and environmental perturbations, and use those predictions to guide rational biological design — <strong>before touching a pipette</strong>.</p>

## Research directions · 研究方向

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

<h3>🧬 Whole-Cell Modelling</h3>
<h4>全细胞模型</h4>

<p>Mechanistic, genome-scale reconstructions integrating <strong>metabolism, gene regulation and growth</strong> in one computable system.</p>

<p>机制驱动的基因组尺度重建：把代谢、基因调控与生长整合进同一个可计算模型。</p>

<p><code>GEM</code> <code>FBA / dFBA</code> <code>Kinetic Models</code> <code>SBML</code></p>

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

<h3>◈ AI Virtual Cell</h3>
<h4>AI 虚拟细胞</h4>

<p>Foundation models trained on single-cell multi-omics to predict cellular responses to <strong>unseen genetic and chemical perturbations</strong>.</p>

<p>基于单细胞多组学训练基础模型，预测细胞对未见过的遗传与化学扰动的响应。</p>

<p><code>Foundation Models</code> <code>Perturbation</code> <code>scRNA-seq</code> <code>Graph Learning</code></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

<h3>◉ Agricultural Digital Twins</h3>
<h4>农业数字孪生</h4>

<p>Digital twins of the rhizosphere, nitrogen-fixing symbionts and crop cells, linking <strong>cellular mechanisms to field phenotype</strong>.</p>

<p>构建根际微生物组、共生固氮体系与作物细胞的数字孪生，连接细胞机制与田间表型。</p>

<p><code>Rhizosphere</code> <code>N₂ Fixation</code> <code>Multi-scale Modelling</code></p>

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

<h3>⟲ Design–Build–Test–Learn</h3>
<h4>DBTL 闭环</h4>

<p>Automated pipelines for part registries, circuit design and <strong>model-guided experiment selection</strong>, feeding evidence back to the bench.</p>

<p>自动化元件库、线路设计与模型引导的实验选择，让数据持续反馈并改进下一轮设计。</p>

<p><code>Part Registry</code> <code>Circuit Design</code> <code>Active Learning</code> <code>Automation</code></p>

</td>
</tr>
</table>
